About Me

Hey!

I'm Yichang, an experience designer based in Singapore.

I studied computer science before settling into the design field. I discovered my interests when I studied Human-Computer Interaction in school. When I interned in the AutoCAD UX design team of Autodesk, I realized how proud it could be to design something that improved the users' life. After graduation, apart from being a software engineer, I also worked on some UX design projects to practice my design skills. I've been lucky enough to have the chance to work with amazing people who encouraged me to keep challenging myself. In 2019, I took the full-time UX design course in the General Assembly, and to be a fulltime UX designer.

I get very proud of myself when I see my designs can solve users' pain and bring some delight to their life.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me either cooking for my family/friends or petting with my cat or someone else dogs/cats/alpaca.

Feel free to reach out for collaborations or just a friendly hello :)
